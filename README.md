# Marketing Unit Economics Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes marketing efficiency and unit economics for a digital product. The goal was to identify the reasons behind declining profitability and evaluate acquisition channel performance.

---

## Business Problem

Despite increasing advertising spend, the company experienced negative ROI across several acquisition channels.

The analysis focused on:
- CAC
- LTV
- ROI
- Retention
- Conversion

---

## Key Findings

- Major acquisition channels (TipTop, FaceBoom) were unprofitable
- CAC increased over time while LTV remained stable
- Organic traffic showed stronger retention
- The US market generated the highest acquisition costs

---

## Recommendations

- Reduce investment in underperforming channels
- Reallocate budget toward profitable acquisition sources
- Improve targeting and campaign optimization
- Monitor CAC/LTV ratio continuously
